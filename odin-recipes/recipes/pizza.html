Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, olives, vegetables, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese, oregano, and garlic powder over the pizza.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
